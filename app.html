GOA -THE STATE OF INDIA

This place is known for its famous beaches, culture, climate, and historical places. You may find colourful architecture, lush greenery, and breathtaking beaches here. Goa is a perfect blend of Portugal and Indian culture. People get attracted to this place to experience world-class music, dance, and sunset.

KERALA -BEAUTIFUL PLACE

Kerala, a state on India's tropical Malabar Coast, has nearly 600km of Arabian Sea shoreline. It's known for its palm-lined beaches and backwaters, a network of canals. Inland are the Western Ghats, mountains whose slopes support tea, coffee and spice plantations as well as wildlife. National parks like Eravikulam and Periyar, plus Wayanad and other sanctuaries, are home to elephants, langur monkeys and tigers.

KODAIKANAL -HILL STATION

Located in the state of Tamil Nadu, Kodaikanal is one of the most famous honeymoon destinations has a beautiful climate, mist-covered manicured cliffs and waterfalls. The gift of the forest and One of the most popular tourist spots of South India, Kodaikanal is blessed with thrilling trekking routes, pristine waterfalls, serene lakes, ancient caves, exquisite temples and grand churches.




MANALI -COOLEST PLACE

Manali is famous for being India's honeymoon capital. It is situated between the snow-capped slopes of the Pir Panjal and Dhauladhar ranges and offers stunning views of lush green forests, green meadows, and meandering blue streams.

VIZAG -LIGHT HOUSE

Visakhapatnam, also commonly known as Vizag, is one of the oldest port cities in the country. Situated in the heart of Andhra Pradesh, Visakhapatnam is known for its picturesque beaches and serene landscape, as well as a rich cultural past, which makes it an ideal spot for a fantastic coastal vacation.
